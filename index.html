About the Firm Our Mandate & Approach

A Legal Institution Built on Discipline and Professional Responsibility

B. N. Ogari & Co. Advocates was established with a clear and abiding purpose: to provide principled, technically sound legal services to those who engage the Kenyan legal system at every level. The firm is led by senior counsel whose practice spans civil and commercial litigation, land law, and advisory work on matters of regulatory significance.

We conduct our work according to the highest standards set by the Law Society of Kenya and the Courts. Our approach is deliberate: we assess each matter thoroughly, advise clearly, and represent clients with disciplined advocacy. We do not make promises without legal foundation, and we pursue every legitimate avenue available under Kenyan law.

Our presence in Kisii positions us to serve clients across the South Nyanza circuit — including in proceedings before the High Court, the Employment and Labour Relations Court, the Environment and Land Court, and the Magistrates' Courts.

I

Fidelity to the Law

Every instruction we accept is evaluated within the full scope of the applicable law. We advise clients honestly, including on the limitations and risks their matters carry.

II

Client Confidentiality

Legal privilege is not a courtesy — it is a professional obligation. All client communications and file contents are held in strict confidence at all times.

III

Institutional Accountability

We maintain clear records, provide clients with regular updates on their matters, and operate within the ethical framework of the Advocates Act and LSK guidelines.

Rooted in Kisii. Engaged across the Nyanza Circuit.

The firm maintains its principal office in Kisii Town, from which we attend proceedings across the South Nyanza region, including courts sitting at Homabay, Migori, and Nyamira. We have established working relationships with correspondents in Nairobi for matters requiring presence before the superior courts and tribunals in the capital.

What We Do

Core Practice Areas

The firm's work is organized around six principal areas of law, each handled by advocates with direct courtroom and advisory experience in the relevant subject matter.

Civil & Commercial Litigation

Representation in civil suits, contract disputes, debt recovery actions, injunctions, and commercial claims before the Magistrates' Courts, the High Court, and the Court of Appeal. We prepare and argue complex procedural and substantive matters with thoroughness.

Learn More

Land & Property Law

Conveyancing, title investigations, transfers and charges, adverse possession claims, succession disputes involving land, and litigation before the Environment and Land Court. We advise clients on land ownership within community land and freehold regimes.

Learn More

Succession & Estate Administration

Filing and prosecution of succession causes, obtaining grants of probate and letters of administration, confirmation of grants, distribution of estates, and contentious probate matters. We advise personal representatives and beneficiaries at every stage.

Learn More

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

Company incorporation, shareholder agreements, contract drafting and review, regulatory compliance advisory, and general commercial transactional support. We advise SMEs and larger enterprises on structuring their legal affairs with clarity and precision.

Learn More

Employment & Labour Relations

Representation of employers and employees before the Employment and Labour Relations Court. We handle wrongful and unfair dismissal claims, redundancy disputes, enforcement of employment contracts, and advise on compliance with the Employment Act, 2007.

Learn More

Small Claims & Dispute Resolution

Advisory and representation in small claims proceedings, alternative dispute resolution, and mediation. We assist clients in resolving disputes efficiently and cost-effectively, particularly where the value or nature of the matter does not warrant protracted litigation.
